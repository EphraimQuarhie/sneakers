Who We Are

Founded in 2019, Element 9 started as a small passion project by sneaker enthusiasts who believed everyone deserves access to premium footwear. What began in a small garage has grown into a trusted destination for sneaker lovers across the globe.

Our name "Element 9" represents the 9 core values we live by: Quality, Authenticity, Style, Innovation, Community, Sustainability, Accessibility, Passion, and Excellence.

Today, we partner with top brands like Nike, Jordan, Vans, and Converse to bring you the latest releases and timeless classics, all backed by our 100% authenticity guarantee.
