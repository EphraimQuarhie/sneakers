Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within Ghana. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available for an additional fee. International orders typically take 10-14 business days depending on the destination.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $100. Orders under $100 have a flat shipping rate of $10 within Ghana.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account or contacting our support team.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for specific rates to your country.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in original packaging. Items must be in new condition with all tags attached. Return shipping is free for exchanges; refunds have a $5 restocking fee.

How do I exchange for a different size?

Contact our support team or initiate an exchange through your account. We'll send you a prepaid return label and ship your new size as soon as we receive the original pair.

What if my sneakers are defective?

We stand behind our products. If you receive defective sneakers, contact us within 48 hours with photos. We'll arrange a free replacement or full refund immediately.

Authenticity & Quality

Are all your sneakers authentic?

100% guaranteed authentic. We source directly from authorized retailers and brand partners. Every pair undergoes our rigorous authentication process. We provide a certificate of authenticity with every purchase.

How do you verify authenticity?

Our expert team checks multiple authentication points including materials, stitching, labels, box quality, and unique identifiers. We use industry-standard verification methods and partner with authentication services.

Payment & Security

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use SSL encryption and never store your full payment details. All transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways.

Sizing & Fit

How do I find my correct size?

Check our size guide on each product page. We recommend measuring your foot length and comparing to our size chart. If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

Do different brands fit differently?

Yes, sizing can vary between brands. Nike tends to run true to size, Jordans often run slightly large, and Vans typically run true to size. Check individual product descriptions for specific fit notes.
