Delivery Information

Where We Ship

We currently ship to all regions in Ghana and internationally to over 50 countries including USA, UK, Canada, Germany, France, and more. Contact us for specific country availability.

Order Processing

Orders placed before 2 PM GMT are processed the same day. Orders placed after 2 PM or on weekends/holidays are processed the next business day. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking once shipped.

Delivery Attempts

Our courier will attempt delivery twice. If unsuccessful, your package will be held at the nearest pickup point for 7 days. Unclaimed packages will be returned to us.

How to Return

  1. Contact us via email or WhatsApp to initiate your return
  2. Receive your prepaid return label (free for exchanges)
  3. Pack items securely in original packaging
  4. Drop off at any courier pickup point
  5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days of receipt
